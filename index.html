￼ Tradeway Story Browse Post Join Your skills are currency. Tradeway is built on a simple idea: people already have value. They just need a way to exchange it. I was going through my wallet one day and realized I needed a new one. The problem wasn’t that I didn’t want one — I just didn’t want to spend money on it. But I had skills. I could build things. I could create value. There has to be someone out there who has what I need… and needs what I can do. Why does everything have to go through money? What if people could trade what they already have — their skills, their time, their effort — directly with each other? Not selling. Not buying. Just value for value. Tradeway is the answer.
