Why Tradeway exists

I was going through my wallet one day and realized I needed a new one.

The problem wasn’t that I didn’t want one — I just didn’t want to spend money.

But I had skills. I could build things. I could create value.

There has to be someone out there who has what I need… and needs what I can do.

Why does everything have to go through money?

What if people could trade skills, time, and effort directly?

Tradeway is the answer.
